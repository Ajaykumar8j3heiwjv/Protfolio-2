Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 2-3 months or more. During our initial consultation, I'll provide you with a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront payment to begin the project, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payments to break the cost into manageable installments. All payment terms are clearly outlined in the project proposal and contract.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance services to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and functioning optimally. Maintenance packages include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates. I also offer on-demand maintenance services for clients who don't need regular maintenance but occasionally require updates or fixes.

Can you help with existing websites or only new ones?

I work with both new websites and existing ones. Whether you need a complete redesign, specific feature additions, performance improvements, or bug fixes for your current website, I can help. I'll first analyze your existing website to understand its structure and identify areas for improvement before proposing solutions.

What technologies do you use for development?

I specialize in modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React.js, Vue.js, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and MySQL. For mobile development, I use React Native and Flutter. The specific technologies used for your project will depend on your requirements, but I always prioritize using the most appropriate and efficient technologies for each unique project.
